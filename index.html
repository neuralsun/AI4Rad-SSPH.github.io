Research Directions

🩻
Medical Vision-Language Models
Developing and clinically validating VLM-based systems for radiology reporting, diagnostic reasoning, and human-AI collaboration in real-world workflows.
🏥
Clinical Use of AI
Translating AI models from bench to bedside through multi-center validation, reader studies, and deployment frameworks for chest, brain, and interventional imaging.
🔄
Cross-Distribution Generalization
Investigating domain generalization, federated learning, and robust representation learning so that clinical AI performs reliably across hospitals, scanners, and patient populations.
🫁
Chest & Neuro Imaging
Building large-scale, multi-source models and benchmarks for chest X-ray/CT analysis and brain MRI report generation.
