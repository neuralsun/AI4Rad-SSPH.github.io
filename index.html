Research Directions

Vision Foundation Models
Visual representation learning for detection, segmentation, and quantitative biomarkers in vascular and musculoskeletal imaging.
Multimodal Large Language Models
Integrating imaging, reports, and clinical data for structured reporting and diagnostic reasoning.
Medical Image Generation
Image synthesis, denoising, and enhancement to improve image quality and reduce scan time.
Spatial Intelligence & Physics Simulation
3D modeling, registration, and simulation for vascular and musculoskeletal analysis.
