Our Scope

AI4Rad Lab is the medical-imaging AI group of the Department of Radiology at Shanghai Sixth People's Hospital. We build on four technical pillars — vision foundation models, multimodal LLMs, medical image generation, and spatial intelligence — and apply them across seven clinical domains, from pan-vascular and musculoskeletal imaging to oncology, chronic-disease cohorts, and emergency radiology. Our north star is simple: technology that reaches the bedside.

Research Directions

👁️
Vision Foundation Models
Medical visual representation and structured understanding for diagnosis, segmentation, detection, localization, and quantitative parameter extraction.
🧠
Multimodal Large Language Models
Integrating images, radiology reports, clinical history, and knowledge bases for report generation, clinical reasoning, evaluation, and agentic AI.
🎨
Medical Image Generation
Cross-modal synthesis, low-dose denoising, image enhancement, fast imaging, and missing-sequence completion.
🌐
Spatial Intelligence & Physics Simulation
3D spatial modeling, registration, hemodynamic simulation, musculoskeletal mechanics, and physics-consistent quantitative analysis.
